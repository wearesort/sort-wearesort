The Sort Code

We are Sort. We're a bit different from your average digital company, but we think that's a good thing.

Together, our team have created a set of guiding principles for our business based on our wealth of experience and shared belief in doing what’s right for our clients, our industry, society, and ourselves.

These combine to form The Sort Code:

Focus on real, desirable outcomes when investing in change
Not instruct, specify or demand the production of highly detailed requirements
100% transparency, speaking plain English, and simple solutions
Not working in silos, using jargon-filled language, and unnecessarily complex solutions
Continuously delivering value through long-term partnerships
Not “one off” short-term projects that ignore their impact on wider business performance
Being flexible and doing what’s needed to do what’s right
Not “doing what’s in my job description” and waiting for bureaucratic sign-off
User needs and flexible outcome-lead approaches
Not “gut feel” assumptions and delivery framework restrictions

As a team, we are bound by and wholeheartedly believe in our principles that we’ve established together. However, we fully understand that as our world changes, so too will our principles. We therefore commit to staying agile as a business, which involves iterating The Sort Code as we progress.
