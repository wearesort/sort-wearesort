About us

We are Sort. We're a bit different from your average digital company, but we think that's a good thing.

At a glance:

  • Our team includes highly skilled consultants, UX/UI & service designers, and developers.
  • We primarily work remotely, spending time on-site with clients as needed. Many of our team members are based in London and around the M25 but we have colleagues in Bristol, Southampton, and Scotland.
  • Collectively, we’ve got lots of experience in a wide variety of industries across private and public sectors.
