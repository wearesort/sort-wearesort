Problem

Prior to undertaking a Discovery, the FSA were already aware of a number of problems:

  • Limited understanding of site users and their needs
  • Limited understanding of effectiveness of various services
  • Negative user feedback from annual surveys
  • Lack of a content strategy or editorial workflow; site has 100,000+ pages
  • Legacy CMS making content production slow and site difficult to iterate
  • Multilingual content issues
  • High costs associated to SMS/email comms and offline contact enquiries
  • Legacy infrastructure performance issues
  • Little agile delivery experience and low technical expertise

Solution

The project started with a x12 week Discovery phase, delivered to GDS design manual standards, including:

  • Strategy, past research and user feedback reviews
  • Website analytics analysis
  • Internal & external stakeholder interviews and workshops
  • User interviews spanning a wide range of user groups, from micro to large businesses undertaking a wide range of food-related activities, to consumers, researchers and Local Authorities
  • User personas
  • Co-creation sessions
  • User journey mapping
  • Content strategy & IA
  • Technology review & recommendations

The Discovery clearly identified x4 key services to focus on during Alpha.

Activity during Alpha:

  • Agile training for key stakeholders
  • Clickable prototyping with several rounds of remote and lab user testing and iteration
  • MVP development of key services to validate technology choice (Drupal) and test legacy system integrations

Following a successful presentation of the Discovery and Alpha findings to the GDS, the project moved into Beta phase:

  • Working with Comms team to adhere to FSA style-guide whilst utilising GDS design patterns
  • Slow roll-out across different user groups, constantly iterating based on feedback, before making Beta site public alongside old site

Results

  • Significantly increased positive feedback from all user groups for all services
  • Overhauled content strategy with enhanced editorial workflows
  • Significantly reduced SMS/email costs through utilisation of GDS Notify
  • Reduction in offline contact enquiries by increasing online contact service usage
  • Fully multilingual site
  • Implemented comprehensive analytics framework and created performance dashboards for future data-oriented site iteration
  • Upskilled FSA team in agile digital delivery
